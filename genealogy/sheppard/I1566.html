Obituary23 May 1946LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Robert Wilson Moncrief
Robert W. Moncrief, son of Robert Wilson and Matilda (Young) Moncrief, was born December 4, 1874 and died May 17, 1946.
He was united in marriage to Eva Lena Lea October 21, 1896. To this union two children were born, a son, Fred E. Moncrief who preceded his father in death December 2, 1928 and a daughter, Mrs. Oren Euritt of LaPlata.
Besides his wife and daughter, he leaves two grandchildren, Robert h. Moncrief of Licking, Missouri and Mrs. George Huffman of LaPlata. Also a daughter-in-law of Licking, Missouri, a son-in-law , Orene L. Euritt leaves two brothers, W.B. Moncrief and D.N. Moncrief of LaPlata and a host of nieces and nephews and friends.
He united with the Christian Church in LaPlata several years ago.
Interment was made in the LaPlata cemetery.
LaPlate Home Press May 23, 1946''
