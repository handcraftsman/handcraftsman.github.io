Court29 Aug 1874Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
"Affidavit of Death, Place of Abode, &c.
In the Surrogate Court of the County of Kent
In the Goods of Matthew Martin deceased.
I, Matthew Martin of the Township of Tilbury East in the County of Kent, yeoman make oath and say that I am the only son of the said Matthew Martin deceased.
That the deceased died on or about the fifteenth day of August in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and seventy-four at the said township of Tilbury East and that the said deceased at the time of his death had his fixed place of abode in at the township of Tilbury East in the county of Kent.
Sworn at Chatham in the county of Kent the 29th day of August A.D. 1874 before me
A. Bell a commisioner in ?? for Kent County
Matthew Martin ''
