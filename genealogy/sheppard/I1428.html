Obituary17 Jul 1913LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Mrs. Elizabeth Snow, the widow of Richard Snow, was born in Knox County, Missouri, July 14, 1842, and died at the home of her daughter, Mrs. Alice Saling, in Calloway County, Missouri, July 13, 1913, aged seventy years, eleven months, twenty-nine days after several years of affliction and suffering, but was kindly and tenderly cared for by her children.
For the last three years she had made her home with her two daughters in Calloway County, Mrs. Cinderella Crow and Mrs. Alice Saling.
She was brought back to her home church, Hazel Dell, in Knox County, where the funeral services wer held July 15, conducted by her old pastor, Elder J. W. Davis of Kirksville, and the body laid to rest in the nearby cemetery to rest till the resurrection morn.
She left two sisters, Mrs. Rebecca Snow and Mrs. Mary Applegate, one half-brother, John Davis, seven children, twenty-six grandchildren, five great-grandchildren besides many relatives and friends to mourn after her.
By. J.W. Davis
LaPlata Home Press July 17, 1913''
