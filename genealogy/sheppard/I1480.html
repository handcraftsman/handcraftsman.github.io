Estate Inventory1 Sep 1789Daulphin Co., PA
"The inventory of his estate was written 1 Sep 1789"
Probate17 Oct 1789Daulphin Co., PA
Daulphin Co. is now Lebanon Co., PA. His will is on file in the Register of Wills Office of the Daulphin County Courthouse, in file 1 (old file). The will was not probated according to County personnel; however, there is a probate date of 17 Oct 1789. The will was accepted by the Register of Wills and is recorded in the index of Wills.
