Obituary4 Oct 1956LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Doctor Norman Moncrief, long time resident of LaPlata, died at his home in Fulton, October 1. He was eighty years of age and had been in ill health since he left LaPlata four years ago.
Mr. Moncrief was the son of R.W. and Matilda Moncrief and was born on a farm east of LaPlata January 9, 1876.
He was united in marriage to Miss Leona Lea who lived only four months after their marriage. In later years he married Mrs. Etta Cabeen who preceded him in death. Sometime later he was married to Mrs. Alice Moncrief who survives.
Mr. Moncrief lived in LaPlata for many years and was actively employed in various kinds of work until he moved to Fulton.
He was a member of the LaPlata Methodist Church which he joined in 1923.
Survivors include his wife, six step-children, Mrs. Lyle Ancell of LaPlata, Mrs. R.B. Dickson of Fulton, Cecil Saling of Mokane, Finis Saling of Fulton, Warner Saling of Portland, Missouri, Mrs. Robert Coperhouer of Clarence and several nieces and nephews.
Funeral services were yesterday afternoon at 2:00 at the Methodist Church, Rev. J.T. Quigley officiated. Christie Funeral Home was in charge. Burial was in the LaPlata Cemetery.
LaPlata Home Press October 4, 1956''
