Witness17 Jul 1733Essex Co., VA
17 Jul 1733 - John Haile Jr witnessed bond of John and Elinor Adcock unto Samuel Peachy.
Mentioned17 Apr 1737Essex Co., VA
17 Apr 1737, will of Jonathan Haile of Essex co., directs that all of Jonathan's lands go to brother John Haile, Jr if Jonathan's son James dies without children.
Court17 Jun 1740Essex Co., VA
17 Jun 1740 - John Haile, Jr, Joseph Burnett and Thomas Burk bound for 200 pounds for John Haile, Jr as guardian of Anabelle Faulkner until orphan shall reach lawful age.
Bond21 Jul 1741Essex Co., VA
21 Jul 1741, Susannah Greenhill, John Haile, Jr and Thomas Haile posted bond of 250 pounds for Susannah Greenhill, admin of the estate of James Greenhill, deceased.
Property18 Oct 1742Essex Co., VA
18 Oct 1742 - John Haile for natural love and fatherly love deeds made to his son John Haile, Jr - 100 acres of his tract of 400 acres.
