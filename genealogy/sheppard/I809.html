Sarah was mentioned in her husband's will dated 21 Apr 1810 in Wilkes co., NC
Member1798Reddies River, Wilkes Co., NC
John and Sarah Shepherd were charter members of the Reddies River Church in 1798. Sarah "withdrew" from the church in 1816.
