Shiloh Cemetery Burial Chronology

This is a chronology of the burials in Shiloh Cemetery.


Cemetery Information

Recorded By Recorded by Rebekah and Michael Sheppard on 21 Dec 2002.
Location Shiloh Cemetery is on a CR 139 about 4.5 miles south of Hutto
Condition This cemetery has short grass. It appears that the east end of the cemetery is being used as a dump/burn location for houshold and field trash.
