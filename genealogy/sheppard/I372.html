BirthAbt 1690
Death23 Sep 1799Louisa Co., VA
MilitaryAbt 1777VA
John Perkins held public service claims for patriot service renderd during the REvolutionary War.
Property1782Louisa Co., VA
owned 280 acres in Louisa co., Va in 1782 based on tax records.
Property3 Mar 1785St. Paul's Parish, Hanover Co., VA
"I Luke Anthony Senr. of St. Paul's Par., Hanover co., do give unto my beloved son in law John Perkins four negros: Joe, James, Hanah, and Jesse and their increase.
dated: 4 Nov 1784
signed: Luke (A) Anthony
wit: John Norvell, Richard Johnson, Hugh Norvell
3 Mar 1785: proved by oath of John Norvell and Hugh Norvell"
Property1790Prob. Hanover Co., VA
Luke Anthony, Sr., left 100 acres of land to John and Mary Perkins by his will in 1790.
Property1797Louisa Co., VA
owned 400 acres in Louisa co., Va in 1797 based on tax records.
Will23 Sep 1799Louisa Co., VA
Will of John Perkins. Gives all to wife Mary until death or remarriage. Mentions decd. son Jesse's children & all my children: Nancy Wilkerson, Michael Perkins, John Perkins, Molly Farr, Anthony Pe rkins, William Perkins, Samuel Perkins, Suky Johnson, Sally Pulliam. Bety Seargeand & my seaid sone Jesse's children: Polly, John, Francis, Betsy, & David. To son Samuel's children by his first wif e: Joseph, Nancy, Jane, Richard & Benjamin. Exors: wife Mary & son John PErkins. Dates 23 Sep 1799. Signed: John Perkins. wit: Wm. G. Poindexter, Dan (X) William, Elizh. (X) Williams. Rcd: 9 De c 1799.
Probate9 Dec 1799Louisa Co., VA
will proved: 9 Dec 1799 in Louisa co., Va
Probate20 Jan 1800Louisa Co., VA
estate of John Perlins appraised by Wm. Price and Reyonlds Henley, and John Price. Appraisement on 20 Jan 1800. Recorded 10 Feb 1800.
