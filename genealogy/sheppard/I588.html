Birth1709Hanover Co., VA
Death1786Granville Co, NC
Spouse<1728>
m. 1st Mary Ann Owen (abt 1691 Henrico co., VA - bef 1740), dau of Thomas woen and Elizabeth Brooks, and had at least: Nancy Ursula (1728), Susannah, Elizabeth, Mary, and Sarah
Spouse
m. 2nd. Frances Grant
PropertyJun 1758Granville Co, NC
"Deed Book C, page 470 reveals the following; Drury Allen, late of Lunenburg Co. from William Gowers of Granville Co. NC., for eighty pounds a tract of 910 acres which Drury and his brother William Allen of Hanover Co., VA. have agreed to divide between them. This was June, 1758"
ResidentApr 1777Granville Co, NC
Was still living in Granville co., Nc in April 1777 as he is mentioned as having property adjoining his brother Drury when that property is sold in april 1777.
