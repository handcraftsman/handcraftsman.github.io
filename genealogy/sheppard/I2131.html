Oral History1834
"Crossed from Switzerland with father and mother in 1834. Father died at
sea. Settled in the Highland, Illinois area. In 1855 traveled by wagon
train with mother and seven other families to Anderson Valley. (Garey,
Gschwend, Houseman, Gossman, Schnider, Guntly, and Conrad all who settled in the lower part of Anderson Valley in 1855.
The family ran a stage stop and store, 12 miles west of Boonville, at
Christine. Both husband and wife homesteaded at Philo (Frank Guntly Ranch).
Mary Barbara Guntly had her first child in Illinois, the others at Christine
on Mill Creek, Anderson Valley."
