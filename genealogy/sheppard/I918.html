Birth<1730>St. George's Parish, Spotsylvania Co., VA
DeathAft 22 Jun 1798Prob. Spotsylvania Co., VA
Mentioned10 Jan 1750St. George's Parish, Spotsylvania Co., VA
George was mentioned in his father's will dated 10 Jan 1750 in St. George's Parish, Spotsylvania co., Va
Property31 Aug 1751St. George's Parish, Spotsylvania Co., VA
on 31 Aug 1751, Benjamin Holloday of Spts. co., and Susanna his wife, to George Shepherd [jr] of same couty. Sd. Holloday having sold to one George Sheperd [sr] departed this life before a conveyance of sd. land was made to him. Sd. Shepherd by his last will and testament, dated 10 Jan 1750, devised the sd. land to his two sons, George [jr] and Robert Shepherd, to be equally divided between them. Under these conditions sd. Holloday and wife convey the sd. property to George Shepherd [jr], son of George Shepard [sr] decd., etc., etc. Witnesses: Zachary Lewis, John Battaley, John Payton. recorded: 1 Oct 1751.
Election1758Spotsylvania Co., VA
did NOT vote for Fielding Lewis in the election of 1758.
Court3 Mar 1764Spotsylvania Co., VA
3 Mar 1764, George Shepherd [jr] witness to transfer of land from John Waller, clerk of Spts co., to Richard Coleman of same county. recorded: 5 Mar 1764.
Property8 May 1764Spotsylvania Co., VA
8 May 1764, John Waller of Spts co., Gent, and Ann his wife, to George Shepherd [jr] of same co. L60 currency 141 acres in St. George's Par, Spts co. Witnesses: John Battaley, William Wood. recorded: 8 May 1764.
Court5 Sep 1769Spotsylvania Co., VA
5 Sep 1769, George Sheppard [jr] was paid L100 with Roger Dixon as administrators for will of Jerrerd Keyton.
Court20 Sep 1770Spotsylvania Co., VA
20 Sep 1770, George Shepperd [jr] witness of land transfer from Benjamin Steward (Stuart), and Sarah his wife, of Westmoreland co. to Thomas Dillard of Berkeley Par., Spts. co. recorded: 2 Sep 1770.
Court10 Jan 1773Spotsylvania Co., VA
10 Jan 1773, George Shepard [jr] witness of land transfer from Robert Huddleston of Berkeley Par, Spts co., and Elizabeth his wife, to Elijah Dismukes of Drisdale Par, Caroline co.
Property21 Apr 1774Berkeley Parish, Spotsylvania Co., VA
21 Apr 1774, John Keeton of St. Geo Par., Spts co., and Elizabeth his wife, to George Shepherd [jr] of Berkeley Par, same co. L50 currency 150 acres in Spts co., and Caroline co., etc. Witnesses: William x Thornton, John x Thornton. recorded: 16 Dec 1774.
CourtAbt Oct 1774Spotsylvania Co., VA
George Shepperd owned land adjoining property of Thomas Dillard per will of Thomas Dillard who d. 23 Oct 1774.
Court5 Dec 1774Spotsylvania Co., VA
5 Dec 1774, George Sheppard [jr] was paid L500 along with John Dillard and Thomas Dillard as guardians of Fielding Dillard.
Property20 Nov 1776Spotsylvania Co., VA
20 Nov 1776, Robert x Shepherd and Sarah his wife, George Sheperd and Mary his wife, of Spts. co., to Benjamin Holloday of same co., L54 currency 108 acres in Spts. co. Witnesses: Charles Yates, Edward Herndon, John Chew, Jr., Anthony G Holston, Clayton Coleman, John Herndon, John Holloday, Jr. recorded: 6 Jan 1779.
Property18 Jun 1778Spotsylvania Co., VA
18 June 1778, George Shepherd [jr] and Mary, his wife, of Spts. co., to William Emerson, Jr. of same county. L75 currency 141 acres in Berkeley Parish, Spts. co. No Witnesses. recorded: 18 June 1778.
CourtAbt Apr 1782Spotsylvania Co., VA
George Shepherd [jr] mentioned in will of Anthony Arnold who d. 7 Apr 1782.
Property9 Sep 1782Spotsylvania Co., VA
9 Sep 1782, Richard Stevens of Caroline co. to George Shepperd [jr] of Spts. co. L200 currency 200 acres in Spts. co., known by name of Downes, and now occupied by Wiliam Darnaby, etc. Witnesses: William Johnston, Jr., G. Baynham, Charles Tod, Joseph Brock, Thomas Towles. recorded: 17 Apr 1783.
Property7 Dec 1790Spotsylvania Co., VA
