Tax1782Wilkes Co., NC
James Shepperd listed on the 1782 Wilkes Co Tax List in Capt. Rowland Judd's District with 110 acres of land, 2 negroes, 4 mules and horses, and 11 cattle.
CourtApr 1784Wilkes Co., NC
