Will<1798><Madison Co., KY>
"The last will and testament of John Phelps Senr. Dec'd Verbaly defined in presents of us John Stapp, Cary Phelps and Stephen Salley, first it is my will and desire that my daughter Nancy Phelps, Rody Phelps and Patsey Phelps should have as much of my personal estate as the rest of my children whom have come of age have received at their coming of age or marring. And likewise my sons Cary Phelps, John Phelps, Sarah Sally, Molley Phelps, Betsey Willis, Tho. Phelps, Shadrach Phelps, Magdalin Stapp and Phillip Phelps all to be made equal one with another out of my personal estate, with what they have already had and the residue of my estate both real and personal to continue in the hands of my wife Mary Phelps until her death and then to be equaly divided amongst the whole of my children and it is my wish and desire that the same may be directed by my son Cary Phelps and Stephen Salley executors.
signed: John Stapp, Stephen Salle, Cary Phelps
At a court held for Madison couty on Tuesday the 7th day of August 1798 this noncupative will of John Phelps Senr dec'd was proved by the oath of John Stapp and Stephen Salle witnesses thereto and ordred to be recorded.
teste: Will. Irvine"
Probate7 Aug 1798Madison Co., KY
