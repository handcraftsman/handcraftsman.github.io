Will5 Nov 1915LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Last Will and Testament
Know all men by these presents:
That I, Jacob J. Kautz of the county of Macon, state of Missouri being of sound mind and memory do make and publish this, my last will and testament, in manner and form following:
First: I order and direct that my executrix hereinafter named pay all my just debts and funeral expenses as soon after my decease as conveniently may be.
Second: After the payment of such funeral expenses and debts, I give device and bequeath all the rest, residue and remainder of my property both real and personal to my beloved wife Augusta Kautz, for her own use and benefit during her natural life.
Third: After the death of my beloved wife, Augusta Kautz I give, device and bequeath to my beloved sons, William J. Kautz and Cleophas Kautz the following described real estate, viz. The north-half of the north-west quarter of section twenty (20), and the east-half of the north-east of the north-east quarter of section Nineteen (19). All in township sixty (60).Range thirteen (13), Macon county Missouri. Share and Share alike.
Fourth: After the death of my beloved wife Augusta Kautz I give, device and bequeath to my beloved daughters, Lydia Beighle, Sophia Snow, Evelyn Moncrief, Elvina Kautz, Dorothy Kautz and the heirs of Matilda McCracken, (now deceased) the following described real estate. Viz. The west-half of the north-east quarter, the east-half of the north-west quarter and the west-half of the north-west quarter of the north-east quarter. All in section nineteen (19), township sixty (60), range thirteen (13), Mason county, Missouri. Share and Share alike. (The heirs of Matilda to receive one-sixth or one share of the same as their mother would have received had she lived).
Fifth: All the personal property remaining after the death of my beloved wife Augusta Kautz to be divided equally (Share & Share Alike) among my beloved sons and daughters above named.
And last I hearby constitute and appoint my beloved wife Augusta Kautz to be the Executrix (without Bond) of this, my last will and testament, revoking and annulling all former wills by me made and ratifying and confirming this and no other to be my last will and testament.
In witness whereof, I have hereto set my hand and seal this the 5th day of November A.D. 1915.
Jacob J Kautz (seal)
Witnesses: E. J. Mairens and S. E. Cash.''
