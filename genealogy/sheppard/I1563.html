ObituaryJul 1942LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Aaron Gideon Moncrief was born October 4, 1871 in Macon County, Missouri. He died at his home in LaPlata and was seventy years, nine month and eight days of age. He was the son of Robert Wilson and Matilda Moncrief.
On October 24, 1893 he was united in marriage to Willie May Francis. To this union three children were born, Mrs. Nina Waters of Marceline, Missouri, Roy r. of Halstead, Kansas and Louise of Brookfield, Missouri.
Besides these who mourn his loss are his wife, four brothers, Joe, Bob and Doc, all of LaPlata, one grandson, Ted Raymond Moncrief of Wichita, Kansas, a step-daughter, Mrs. Dessie Hayden of Overton, Kentucky, a step-son, Elmer Francis of Novelty, many nieces and nephews. A granddaughter, Peggy Lou Fleshman, preceded him in death.
Early in life he became a member of the Christian Church at Hazel Dell later, with his wife, moving his membership to this church and was a member here at the time of his death.
LaPlata Home Press July 1942''
