Birth4 May 1794Knob Fork, Grayson Co., VA
Death15 Oct 1882<Elk Creek, Grayson Co., VA>
Tax1824Grayson Co., VA
William Bourn Jr. paid $1.42 tax for the following personal property in 1824 in Grayson co., Va:
1 white tithable
1 black tithables
1 slaves
4 horses
Tax1828Grayson Co., VA
William Bourn Jr. paid $2.48 tax for the following personal property in 1828 in Grayson co., Va:
1 white tithable
3 black tithables
1 negroes age 12+
5 horses
Spouse1832
m. Mary "Polly" Johnstone (10 Jun 1796-30 Aug 1879), dau. of Samuel Johnstone and Mary Hamon, and had descendants

5 horses
