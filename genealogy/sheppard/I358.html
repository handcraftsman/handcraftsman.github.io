They resided on Canal St. until Mrs. Magee became semi-invalid. They sold the home and moved in with their daughter Edith and family on a farm on the edge of Tilbury, where they resided until their death, Agnes in her seventies and Henry in his Eighties.
Funeral24 Dec 1928Chatham, Kent co., Ontario, Canada
"In loving memory of Mrs. Henry Magee who passed away at her home, Ella Street, Tilbury, Ont., on Saturday December 22, 1928, aged 77 years, 7 months, 21 days.
The funeral will take place from the home to St. Andrew's Church, on Monday, Dec. 24, at 3:30 p.m.
Interment in Maple Leaf Cemetery"
Obituary24 Dec 1928Chatham, Kent co., Ontario, Canada
"Mrs. Henry Magee.
Tilbury, December, 24 - Mrs. Henry Magee's passing took place early Friday morning after years of semi-invalidism due to heart trouble. She passed peacefully away in the midst of her family at the home of her daughter Mrs. Newton Ainslee who has been her faithful and constant attendant throughout years of illness. Mrs. Magee passed away six hours after her only brother, Samuel Warnock. She also was a native of North Ireland. She is survived by her husband, Henry Magee, two daughers, Mrs. Ainslie and Mrs. A. Shaufler this town, and two sons, J. A. Magee, Windsor, and James. H. Magee, Calgary, Alberta"
