Obituary15 Sep 1904LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Mary E Graves, wife of Gamaliel H. Graves, departed this life near Warrenton, Missouri, September 6, 1904. She was born in Adair county, near La Plata, April 2, 1864 and was the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. R. W. Moncrief.
At the age of twenty years she became a christian and united with the New Harmony congregation of the Cumberland Presbyterian church near La Plata from whic place she removed her membership to the LaPlata community Presbyterian Church February 14, 1904. On February 23, 1890, the deceased was united in marriage to G. W. Graves.
She was the mother of three children, two boys and one girl. Her two sons preceded her to the better world.
She was laid to rest in the LaPlata cemetery September 8, 1904, beside the loved ones who had preceded her.
There remains to mourn her departure, a husband, one daughter, a mother, one sister and five brothers and a host of friends.
In her death LaPlata loses one of its best mothers and noblest wifes. ï¿½''
