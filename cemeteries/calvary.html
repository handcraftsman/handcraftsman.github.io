Cemetery Information

Recorded By Recorded by Michael and Michelle Sheppard on 18 Jan 2003.
Transcribed By Michelle Sheppard
Location The cemetery is located west of Granger, Texas.
Condition This cemetery grounds are well maintained. Many of the markers have dates in Czechoslovakian and Polish, we have chosen to transcribe these without translation as the language may be a useful clue for the researcher.
