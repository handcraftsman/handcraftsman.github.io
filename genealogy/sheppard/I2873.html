Moved<1799><Northwest Territory>
``Roberts Williams; the latter a native of Kentucky, and who left Kentucky, when two years old, with his father, Remembrance Williams, and migrated to Greene county, Ohio''
note: Ohio didn't exist until 1803. Greene county was created in 1803 from Ross and Hamilton counties of the former NW Territory.
