Hunt Cemetery Burial Chronology

This is a chronology of the burials in Hunt Cemetery.


Cemetery Information

Recorded By Photographed by Michael and Rebekah Sheppard on 9 June 2001.
Location The cemetery is located on county road 258 north-east of Seward Junction.
Condition This cemetery is well maintained. Several older stones have been patched/glued back together. There are a number of graves marked only with field stones or the bottom few inches of an old marker.
