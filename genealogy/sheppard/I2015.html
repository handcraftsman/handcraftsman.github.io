Events

Birth1680Wolfsheim, Baden, Germany
Death1761Skippack, Philadelphia co., PA
ImmigrationJan 1707-Mar 1707PA
Martin came with his brothers Johannes and Jacob to Pennsylvania in the spring of 1707.
Spouse19 May 1709Germantown, Philadelphia co., PA
m. Magdalena Van_Sintern (1687 - aft 1771), dau of Isaac Van Sintern and Neeltje Classen, on 19 May 1709 in Germantown, Philadelphia co., PA and had: Magdalena, Neltgen, Hanna, Sarah, Dielman, Isaac, and Maria
Property1724Skippack, Philadelphia co., PA
Purchased 50 acres in Skippack from Mathias VANBEBBER 1724
PropertyAft 1724Skippack, Philadelphia co., PA
Purchased a 50 acre tract in Skippack from Ludwig SPROGEL aft. 1724
Naturalized1730<Montgomery co., PA>
Naturalized along with Hans (John) DETWEILER, Valentine HUNSICKER, Dielman KOLB, Jacob KOLB, Michael ZIEGLER and others from the Montgomery County area 1729/30
Property1737Salford, Philadelphia co., PA
Purchased 70 acres in Lower Salford from James STEEL 1737
Will12 Jun 1749
will named wife Magdalena. Named children, Isaac, Dielman, Magdalena BECHTEL, Cornelia DETWEILER, Sarah RITTENHOUSE, Mary FRIED and Hannah. dated 12 June 1749
Probate17 Nov 1761
will probated 17 November 1761
