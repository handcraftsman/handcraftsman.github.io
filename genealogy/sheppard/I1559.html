Obituary17 Feb 1916LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Mrs. Mary Parsons passed away at the home of her parents, Mr. And Mrs. G. W. Moncrief, Sunday morning about 2:30 at the age of twenty-five years, eleven months and three days. She had been ill about two weeks with pneumonia.
She was married about four years ago to Bert Parsons. To this union three children were born, all of whom died in infancy.
She leaves to mourn her, a husband, father, mother, three brothers and nine sisters besides many other relatives and friends.
Funeral services were held at the New Harmony Church Monday at 11 am, conducted by Rev. W. H. Johnson of Callao. Interment was in the New Harmony cemetery.
LaPlata Home Press
Feb 17, 1916''
