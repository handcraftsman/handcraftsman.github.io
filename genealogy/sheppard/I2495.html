Will19 Jun 1897Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
Tilbury June 19, 1897
I William Warnock of the Township of Tilbury East in the County of Kent. Gentleman being of sound and disposing mind and memory do make and publish this my last will and testament hereby revoking all former wills by me at any time heretofore made.
1st. I hereby constitute and appoint J.S. Richardson of the village of Tilbury in the County of Kent my sole Executor of my last will directing my said Executor to pay all my just debts and funeral expendses and the legacies hereinafter given out of my estate.
2nd. After the payment of my said debts, and fuenral expenses, I bequeath all my real and personal estate to my son Samuel Warnock to be paid to him within one year after my decease.
Signed sealed published and declared by the said William Warnock the testator as and for his last will and testament in thie presence of us who at his request in his presence and in the presence of each other have hereinto subscribed our names as witnesses to the due execution thereof: John Fergusson, John Kelly.
Probate1 Dec 1897Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
Probate for estate of William Warnock late of the township of Tilbury East in the County of Kent, Gentleman, Deceased
William Warnock died 22 Nov 1897 in Tilbury East, his will dated 19 Jun 1897
