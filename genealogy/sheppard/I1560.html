Obituary30 Apr 1936LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Robert Wilson Moncrief was born December 18, 1895 near Sue City, Missouri, passing away April 20, 1936, being at the time of death, forty years, five months, and height days of age.
He was the son of Garrett and Mary Moncrief. He was married December 22, 1926 to Maggie McNew. To this union her born five children, Howard Dale, Nancy Jane, Pauline, Clifford Gregory and Billy; also one step-daughter, Helen Lucille McNew. He also leaves to mourn, his father and mother, aged grandmother and ten brothers and sisters, Anna Davold, Jessie McGlothlin, Ft. Madison, Iowa, Pearl Hubbs, Kahoka, Eva Delaney, Chicago, Alice Williams, Cole City, Illinois, Bula Jennings, Leona Hardwick of Mpberly (sic), Missouri, Norene and Jack of California, and Willie and Clarence of LaPlata, and a number of nieces and nephews and a host of friends.
Funeral services were held Tuesday afternoon at the Christie Funeral Home at 2:00, conducted by Dannie Martin, pastor of the Christian Church and burial was made at the Stuckey cemetery near Millard.
LaPlata Home Press April 30, 1936''
