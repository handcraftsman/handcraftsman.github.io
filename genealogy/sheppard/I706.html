Birth3 Jun 1794Reddies River, Wilkes Co., NC
Death18 Feb 1849Wilkes Co., NC
BuriedShepherd Cemetery, Reddies River, Wilkes Co., NC
Military1812Wilkes Co., NC
served in the 5th regiment from Wilkes County, NC under Capt. Ambrose Carleton during the war of 1812.
his military record:
6tf tall, blue eyes, fair skin and light brown hair.
Spouse8 Nov 1817Wilkes Co., NC
m. Alley Irwin (6 Mar 1797 - 28 Jun 1879), dau of Elijah Irwin, on 8 Nov 1817, and had: John F., Lucinda, Rebeccah, James Welborn, Franklin, Sarah, Jordan Ashely, Benjamin, Abraham, Martha, and Mary.
Member14 Feb 1818Reddies River, Wilkes Co., NC
Larkin Shepherd was baptised as a member of the Reddies River Church on 2nd Saturday in Feb 1818.
Appointed8 Sep 1821Reddies River, Wilkes Co., NC
was appointed Church Treasurer of the Reddies River, Wilkes co., NC on 2nd saturday in Sep 1821.
ElectionMar 1830Reddies River, Wilkes Co., NC
Larkin Shepherd was chosen as a descon of the Reddies River Church in March 1830.
