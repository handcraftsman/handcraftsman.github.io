Mentioned20 Jan 1768PA
``At a Meeting of the Governors on Wednesday the 20th Jan'ry 1768.
present:
The Governor
The Sec're Mr. Tlighman
The Rec'r Gen'l Mr. Hockley
Catharine Gimbling widow of Bernard Gimbling
agt Baltzer Knertzer & Mich'l Quiggle
On hearing it appeard that Baltzer Knertzer in May 1750 paid unto Geroge Stevenson L5 to obtain him a Warrant for 100 A's of land, and that afterwards the 10th Sept'r following the Warrant was obtained. That in Nov'r of the same or the following year Baltzer Kneetzer (sic) agreed with Simon Melhorn & Adam Crumling for the purchase of the land surveyed under the Warrant which in the agreement is expressed to be about 150 A's more or less. [...] the Governor Orders that the Surveyor General when next he goes to York do survey to Catherine Gimbling in trust for herself & the children of her husband Bernard Gimbling the quantity of 150 A's to include the improvements [...] and that in the mean time the survey of Knertzer made in virtue of his Warrant of the 10th Sept'r 1753 be not received but suspended.''
