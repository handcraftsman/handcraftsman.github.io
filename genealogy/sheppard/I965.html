Birth1675VA
Death3 Sep 1744Essex Co., VA
Property1 Nov 1696Essex Co., VA
1 Nov 1696, Richard Haile deeded 120 acres to his son John Haile, for love and affection, in Essex co., Va, that Richard had obtained by patent. recorded: 22 Nov 1697.
note: John must have been at least 21 years old in 1696 to be able to own land. --gjr
Court27 Jul 1699Essex Co., VA
27 Jul 1699, the estate of John Woods lists accounts and debts due to several persons, including 40 pounds to Capt. Richard Haile and 60 pounds to John Haile. Only John signed the document acknowledging the debt.
Property10 Jul 1703King And Queen Co., VA
10 Jul 1703 - John Haile, Jr deeded 100 acres in King and Queen co., Va, part in Essex co., Va "part of a tract granted to my father, Capt. Richard Haile" to John Loving for 2000 pounds of tobacco).
Tax1704Essex Co., VA
1704, on rent roll for Essex co., Va charged with 900 acres.
Court11 Jun 1706Essex Co., VA
11 Jun 1706, Inventoried the estate of Simon Cobnell with James Fullterton, John Meador and John Gatewood.
Property10 Jun 1708South Farnham Parish, Essex Co., VA
10 June 1708 - John Haile of Southfarnham Parish, Essex co., to Adam Denning of same. for 2700 pounds of tobacco. 252 acres in parish aforesaid... Beverley's Run... head of a small branch... Mr. Robert Beverley by Portobago path... James Boughan... John Loving's and William Alsup's... part of 720 acres, 450 part thereof granted to Theophilus Whale and Daniel Sullivant on 23 Sept 1674 and the residue being overplus in the bounds of the patent and now doth belong unto John Haile the only son and heire apparent unto Richard Haile.
signed John Haile.
witn: Salvator Muscoe, Richard Meaders.
10 June 1708 - Acknowledged by John Haile. Right of dower relinquished by Mary, his wife.
8 June 1708 - Livery and seizin granted. Witnesses: James Boughan, James Boughan, Jr.
10 June 1708 - bond of John Haile of Southfarnham Parish, Essex county, to Adam Denning to same. For 5400 pounds of tobacco. To keep all covenants in the deed.
signed John Haile.
witnesses: Salvator Muscoe, Richard Meader.
10 June 1708 - Acknowledged.
Court13 May 1714Essex Co., VA
Sarah Fullerton made bond on 13 May 1714 for 155 pounds of tobacco with James Griffin. Signers of the bond were Sarah Fullerton, James Griffin, John Picket, John Haile, William Picket.
Witness16 Apr 1716Essex Co., VA
16 Apr 1716 - John Haile witnessed the will of Benjamin Fisher, South Farnham Parish. Probated 19 Jun 1716.
Witness10 Dec 1716Essex Co., VA
10 Dec 1716, John Haile witnessed the will of Richard Dudley of South Farnham Parish.
Property20 Apr 1724Essex Co., VA
20 Apr 1724, John & Mary Haile sold 200 acres of land to John Edmondson. Part of the land was in Essex and part in King and Queen co. Land bounded on one side by land of Jonathan Haile.
Court19 Jul 1726Essex Co., VA
19 Jul 1726 - John Haile, Thomas Meeder, William Allen inventoried the estate of Elizabeth Clinton. Thomas Bell, Admin.
Witness17 Jul 1730South Farnham Parish, Essex Co., VA
17 Jul 1730 - John Haile witnessed will of Richard Gatewood of South Farnham Parish. Proven by John Haile and Thomas clinton 15 Sep 1730.
Gift18 Oct 1742Essex Co., VA
18 Oct 1742 - John Haile for natural love and fatherly love deeds made to his three sons:
to son John Haile, Jr - 100 acres of his tract of 400 acres.
to son Thomas Haile, 120 acres of his 400 acre tract
to son Benjamin Haile, 180 acres of his 400 acre tract.
Will17 Aug 1744Essex Co., VA
will of John Haile, dated 17 Aug 1744 in South Farnham Parish, Essex co., Va
"Undo my well beloved wife the use of all my real and personal estate during her life and widowhood and after the decease of my wife or at the day of her second marriage, to be qually divided amongst my six children now living: Francis Haile, John Haile, Thomas Haile, Benjamin Haile, Mary Edmondson and Susanna Allen. John Haile (son) executor. Witnesses: James Allen, Andrew Allen. recorded: 16 Oct 1744.
Probate16 Oct 1744Essex Co., VA
