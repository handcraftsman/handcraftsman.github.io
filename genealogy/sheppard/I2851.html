Obituary15 Oct 1903LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Mrs. Elizabeth Petree, nee Moncrief, was born in Indiana March 11, 1834 and died in Macon County, Missouri October 8, 1903, aged sixty-nine years, six months, and twenty-seven days.
She was married to Andrew Jackson Petree May 15 1850, and to this union eight children were born, fours sons and four daughters; one son died in infancy.
She moved to Missouri in 1867 and her husband died soon after, and was the first one buried at the New Harmony cemetery.
She joined the Cumberland Presbyterian Church in 1871 and later the Christian Church, and held her membership at Star church. She was buried at New Harmony.
The funeral services were conducted by Elder J. W. Davis of Kirksville and was attended by a large number of sympathizing sorrowing people which showed the high esteem in which she was held by her neighbors where she had lived for so long.''
