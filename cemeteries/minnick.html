Minnick Cemetery Burial Chronology

This is a chronology of the burials in Minnick Cemetery.


Cemetery Information

Recorded By Photographed by Michael and Rebekah Sheppard on 9 June 2001.
Location The cemetery is located in a subdivision in northeast Cedar Park on the corner of Turnbow and Settlement. Go east on FM 1431 from the intersection of US183 1 mile. Turn right on Discovery Drive and go .5 mile. Turn left into the Quest II subdivision and go straight to the end of the road. Go right on Settlement and to the end of the road. This is the corner of Turnbow and Settlement, the cemetery is situated between two houses. Minnic Cemetery on WikiMapia
Condition The Minnick Family Cemetery Association has been in place since 2001 and ensures regular maintenance. In addition to the stones transcribed below, there are three graves that have a marker without inscription.
