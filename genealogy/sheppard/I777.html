ObituarySep 1938<Shannon Co, MO>
"Rachel Isabelle Sheppard was born in Marion co, IL Nov 5, 1892 and died Sep 13, 1938 at the age of 45 years, 10 mo, 8 days.
At the age of 14 years she was united with the Fairman Baptist Chruch at Fairman, IL, holding her membership in goog standing until about a year ago when she moved it to the Oak Grove Baptist Chruch near Bartlet, Mo.
On Sept 23, 1919 she was united in marriage to Walter Edward Sheppard and to this union was born 4 sons, one of which preceded her in infancy. She also has two children by a former marriage, Mrs. Thelma Wright and Claude Henry Snyder.
Funeral Services were held at the Oak Grove Church with Rev. Gordon Napier officiating. Interment was made in the Oak Grove Baptist Cemetery beside the church."
