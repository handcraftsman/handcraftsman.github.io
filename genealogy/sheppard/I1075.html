Will20 Oct 1730Baltimore Co., MD
Will of Sarah Day [wife of Nicholas Sr] dated October 20, 1730, proved December 28, 1736, on file in the Hall of Records (BALTIMORE CO. WILLS, Box 5, folder 87). Bond was made by Nicholas Day [Jr], Josiah Henden, and Thomas Amos (TESTAMENTARY PROCEEDINGS, Lib. 30, fol. 262). Transcription:
Baltemore County In the Name of God Amen I Sarah Day of the County Afosd. being very Sick & Weak but of perfect and Sound Memory thanks be to God for it.
First I desire that all my lawfull debts be Justly paid.
Item I Give & bequeath unto my Daughter Dinah Merica the bed which I always lay on my trunk a warming pan & porrage pott & Palett And I desire that the Rest of my Goods & Chattels be Eaqually Divided between My Son Nicholas Day My Son Edward Day. & My Daughter Sarah Greer the wife of John Greer & my daughter Dinah Merica.
as Witness whereof I have Sett my hand this 20th day of October 1730
Sarah SD Day [her mark]
Testis: James Jones
John Savory [his mark]
