Obituary30 Nov 1961LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Wilhelmina McCracken
Funeral services wer held last Friday at 2 p.m. at the Wilson Funeral Chapel in LaPlata for Mrs. Wilhelmina McCracken who died last Tuesday evening, November 21, at St. Mary's Hospital in Jefferson City.
Mrs. McCracken was born September 18, 1891 at Independence, Iowa and spent her adult live in the LaPlata area. She was married to Clarence E. McCracken who preceded her in death about 15 years ago.
For the past five years Mrs. McCracken had made her home with her daughter, Miss June McCracken in Jefferson City, Missouri.
Survivors include two daughters, Miss June McCracken and Mrs. Melvin (Gertrude) Gardner of Jefferson City, Missouri and one son, Clarence J. McCracken of Elmhurst, Illinois, and also eight grandchildren.
LaPlata Home Press November 30, 1961''
