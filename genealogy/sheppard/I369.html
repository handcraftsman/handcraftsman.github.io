Member1818Reddies River, Wilkes Co., NC
Wiley Shepherd was baptised as a member of the Reddies River Church in 1818.
Census1830Columbia, Adair co., KY
Witness5 Mar 1833Adair Co., KY
John R. Blair m. Elizabeth Demoss on 5 Mar 1833 in Adair co., KY by Sinclair. Attest: Wiley Shepherd.
Resident1835TN
Wiley (Wm.) Sheppard and his wife Rachel, (Peabody) lived in Tennessee where she died and was buried.
Moved1836Near Shobonier, Wilberton Twp., Fayette Co., IL
After Rachel's death, William and son Garland Carter came to Illinois walking from Tennessee. They were carrying their belongings in a bundle on their backs. They settled in Wilberton township, in Fayette co., north of Shobonier, Illinois.
Property1838Adair Co., KY
Records in Adair co., KY show that Wiley Shepherd and Benjamin Carter bought a wool carding factory on Glasgow Road in 1838.
Resident1839Marshall Co., TN
by spring of 1839, Wiley lived in Marshall co., TN
Census1840Marshall Co., TN
Court1843Adair Co., KY
Benjamin Carter brought a suit in Adair Circuit Court about the wool carding factory and bought Wiley's interest in 1843.
Witness15 Dec 1847Fayette Co., IL
Garland C. Sheppard and Elida Allgood, L 15 Dec 1847, he of age, she by con, M 15 Dec 1847 by Elisha Pruett, JP Peter Allgood, Elida's father gave con. Wit: Wiley Sheppard
