Moved12 Jun 1838Gentry Co, MO
He moved to Gentry co., Missouri from Ohio in 1838 with Robert DuBois. They came by boat to Peoria, Illinois where they met Joe Kingsborough, Ira and Alfred Orton. They bought a two horse wagon and they all came together arriving at Gentryville, Missouri June 12, 1838. William homesteaded some 400 acres of land in north central Gentry county.
