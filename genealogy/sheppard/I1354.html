Obituary30 Oct 1919Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
"The late Mrs. John Sinclair (1861-24 Oct 1919). The Funeral of the late Mrs. John Sinclair took place from her hom near Fletcher to Stewart Cemetery on Friday last [10/24/1919] and was largely attended by sorrowing relatives and friends. Deceased who was a daughter of the late Mr. and Mrs. James Campbell, Tilbury East, was in the 58th year of her age and leaves to mourn her loss a sorrowing husband, two sons, Chester and James D, at home, and one daughter, Mrs. John Smith of Tilbury East, also 4 brothers: Mathew, James W. and John I. all of Alberta, Charles of Tilbury East, and one sister Mrs. David L. gardiner of Tilbury East."
