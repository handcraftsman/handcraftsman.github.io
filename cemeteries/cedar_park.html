Cedar Park Cemetery Burial Chronology

This is a chronology of the burials in Cedar Park Cemetery.


Cemetery Information

Recorded By Photographed by Michael and Rebekah Sheppard on 19 May 2001.
Location The cemetery is located on the west side of US 183 about half way through Cedar Park. Cedar Park Cemetery on WikiMapia
Condition This is a well maintained cemetery. The only problem I can see is that the lawnmower crew appears to have hit and broken several funeral markers, spilling the letters and numbers.
