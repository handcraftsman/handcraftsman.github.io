Will17 Apr 1867Vernon twp., Jennings co., IN
"In the name of God Amen. I Caleb Moncrief of the township of Vernon county of Jennings and state of Indiana being of sound and disposing mind and memory praised be God for the same and being desirous of settling my worldly affairs while I have capacity so to do, do make and publish this my last will and testament. that is to say (first) all my just debts are to be paid. (second) I give and bequeath to my beloved wife Jane Moncrief all my estate real and personal of which I may die seazed or possessed during her natural life. (third) after her death I give and bequesth all my real and personal estate to my son Maxa Moncrief. the cause of giving all my estate to my son Maxa Moncrief is in consideration of his and his families kind attention during the long and severe affliction of my wife and myself. (fourth) I hereby appoint my son Gideon Moncrief as my executor.
Caleb Moncrief (seal)
Signed and sealed by William F. Mills at the request of said Caleb Moncrief as his last will and testament in the presence of us who in his presence and in the presence of each other and at his request have hereunto subscribed our names as witnesses.
B. D. Amsden
Sarah Mills
William Mills
Vernon township Jennings Co and State of Indiana this 17th day of April 1867"
