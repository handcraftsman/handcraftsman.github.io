Census21 Jul 1860Cambridge City, Wayne Co., IN

Spouse25 Oct 1877Cambridge City, Wayne Co., IN
m. Edwin B. Fosdick (Mar 1851 OH-) on 23 Oct 1877 in Wayne co., IN and had 5 ch:: Horace W, George H, Edna B, Florence G, and Edwin Clair
``Married, on the 25th inst. at the residence of the bride's father in this city, Mr. Edwin b. Fosdick and Miss Clara r. Weast, Elder C. G. Bartholomew of the Christian Church officiating. The happy pair left at 4 o'clock, same day, for Indianapolis and Chicago on a weeks' pleasure trip, but did not forget the printers, as the supply of wedding cake testified.''
Census24 Jun 1880Cambridge City, Wayne Co., IN
Census7 Jun 1900Indianapolis, Marion Co., IN
Mentioned1 Oct 1908Cambridge City, Wayne Co., IN
``Among those from a distance in attendance at the funeral of Mrs. Geo. L. Weast Sunday, was her son, Robert Elder, Sr., and Robert Elder, jr, and family of Indianapolis; Mr. and Mrs. Ben Stalker, who left the show to Michigan; Ad Sullivan and wife, of Urbana, Ohio; Chas. Davis and wife, of Marion; George L. Weast, Scott Weast and wife and George Weast, jr., and wife, of Springfield, Ohio, and Mr. And Mrs. Ed Fosdick, Indianapolis.''
Census22 Apr 1910Indianapolis, Marion Co., IN
Mentioned11 Sep 1919Cambridge City, Wayne Co., IN
``George Weast and wife and Mr. Tremble and wife of Springfield, Ohio, were here Sunday with Mr. and Mrs. Horace Hamilton, enroute home from a week's visit with Mr. and Mrs. Edward Fosdick and Geo. Weist, sr., and taking in the fair at Indianapolis.''
Mentioned20 Oct 1919Cambridge City, Wayne Co., IN
``George Weast and son, enroute Saturday from Springfield, Ohio, to Indianapolis to visit his aged father Geo. L. Weast, spent a few hours here with old friends, who enjoyed his visit even though it was brief. His father who will be 90 years old in November, is in rather feeble health. He is one of the few men living who served in both the Mexican and Civil wars. His home for several years has been with his daughter Mrs. Ed. Fosdick in Indianapolis.''
Census2 Jan 1920Indianapolis, Marion Co., IN
Census10 Apr 1930Indianapolis, Marion Co., IN
Mentioned19 Jun 1930Cambridge City, Wayne Co., IN
``Mrs. E. B. Fosdick of Indianapolis visited Mrs. J. C. Dodson Tuesday while enroute to Springfield, Ohio, to attend the funeral of her brother Scott Weast, a former resident of this city.''
