Birth21 Jul 1826Ballymoney, County Antrim, Ireland
Death25 Jan 1899Grundy Center, Grundy co., IA
BuriedOak Hill Cemetery, Parkersburg, Butler co., IA
Spouse4 Jan 1855<Tilbury, Kent co., Ontario, Canada>
m. Janet (Jessie) Mann (Aug 1830 Elgin, Scotland - 20 Jan 1911 Goldfield, Iowa), dau of William and Anna Mann, and had: Charles (1855), Richard, Alexander James, Charles, James, William Henry, John Edward, Anna, Margaret, Fredrick, and Joseph (1873)
Property<1848><Tilbury, Kent co., Ontario, Canada>
Charles located on one-half of Lot 6 M.R.N.
Property4 Feb 1853Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
As the executor for his father, Andrew Campbell extr and eldest son of Charles Campbell sold 100 acres comprising N 1/2 of Lot 9 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp to [his brothers] William H. Campbell (E 1/2 of N 1/2 50 acres) and Charles Campbell (W 1/2 of N 1/2 50 acres) for L25 each on 4 Feb 1853.
Property15 Mar 1854Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
bought 100 acres comprising S 1/2 of Lot 5 Con 8 of Tilbury East twp from Edward Keinen for L112.10.0 on 15 Mar 1854 recorded 10 Jun 1854. Edward Keinen received it as a land patent from the Crown on 9 Dec 1847
Property2 Sep 1854Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
sold 100 acres comprising S 1/2 of Lot 5 Con 8 of Tilbury East twp to [his brother] Isaac Campbell for L150 on 2 Sep 1854.
Property24 Mar 1855Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
Charles Campbell sold 50 acres comprising W 1/2 of N 1/2 of Lot 9 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp to [his brother] William H. Campbell for L150 on 24 Mar 1855.
Property3 Dec 1859Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
Charles Campbell bought 25 acres comprising E 1/4 of N 1/2 of Lot 9 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp from [his brother] William H. Campbell for $300 on 3 Dec 1859 recorded 24 Dec 1859.
Property30 Mar 1867Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
Charles Campbell sold 25 acres comprising E 1/4 of N 1/2 of Lot 9 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp to [his brother] James Campbell for $300 on 30 Mar 1867.
Moved<Apr 1867-Apr 1870>Grundy co., IA
moved between Apr 1867 and Apr 1870 (ch birth dates) from Tilbury East, Kent co., Ontario to Grundy co., Iowa
Obituary of their son James (b. 1857) says "when about 9 years of age he moved with his parents to Grundy County, Iowa"
Naturalized12 Nov 1873Grundy Center, Grundy co., IA
"Naturalization: Nov 12 1873, Grundy Center, IA"
