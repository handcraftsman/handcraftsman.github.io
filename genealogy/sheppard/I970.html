Property17 Aug 1752Essex Co., VA
on 19 aug 1752, Thomas and wife Elizabeth sold to John Latane for 5 Shillings, part of 200 acres given to Thomas by his father by deed dated 18 Oct 1742. covered by a mortgage, and the other part that Thomas Haile purchased from his brother Benjamin of South Farnham PArish, Essex co., Va. John Latane was to hold the land for 12 months, an dif requested to do so, was to give an ear of Indian Corn at the Feast of St. Michael. witnessed: James Mills, John Figgins, and Thomas Coleman, Jr.
Will14 Nov 1787Essex Co., VA
will of Richard Thomas Haile dated: 14 Nov 1787 in Essex co., Va
probated: 20 Oct 1795
Refers to wife Mary, sons: John, Lewis, Benjamin, Richard. Daugher Joanna, grandson Richard Thomas Haile, refers to son John's wife as living but does not give her name. Exers: wife Mary and son John.
Witnesses: Thomas allen, Susannah Adams, Mary Croxton.
