Canadian Army Active Discharge Certificate
This is to certify that No L41254 Signalman Norman Eugene McCormick enlisted in the Royal Canadian Corps of Signals, A.F. Canadian Army Active at Kingston, Ontario on the 10th day of April 1941. He served in Royal Canadian Corps of Signals, A.F. and is now discharged from the service by reason of C.A.R.O. 1029 (10), "Unable to meet the required Military Physical Standards"

Will
He did not have a will.
At the time he died, he owned nothing. He had sold the house to Carl at least a year before he and died. My mother automatically began receiving a small union pension and part of his social security.
