Birth<1828><Ballymoney, County Antrim, Ireland>
Death<May-Dec 1859>Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
Property4 Feb 1853Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
As the executor for his father, Andrew Campbell extr and eldest son of Charles Campbell sold 100 acres comprising N 1/2 of Lot 9 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp to [his brothers] William H. Campbell (E 1/2 of N 1/2 50 acres) and Charles Campbell (W 1/2 of N 1/2 50 acres) for L25 each on 4 Feb 1853.
Property24 Mar 1855Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
William H. Campbell bought 50 acres comprising W 1/2 of N 1/2 of Lot 9 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp from [his brother] Charles Campbell for L150 on 24 Mar 1855.
Property23 May 1859Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
William H. Campbell sold 50 acres comprising W 1/2 of N 1/2 of Lot 9 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp to [his brother] James Campbell for $600 on 23 May 1859.
Property2 Dec 1859Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
William H. Campbell willed 25 acres comprising E 1/4 of N 1/2 of Lot 9 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp to [his brother] James Campbell on 2 Dec 1859 recorded 20 Dec 1859.
cites: Will book p 122, will # 84.
Property3 Dec 1859Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
William H. Campbell sold 25 acres comprising E 1/4 of N 1/2 of Lot 9 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp to [his brother] Charles Campbell for $300 on 3 Dec 1859 recorded 24 Dec 1859.
