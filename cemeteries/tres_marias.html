Las Tres Marias Cemetery Burial Chronology

This is a chronology of the burials in Las Tres Marias Cemetery.


Cemetery Information

Recorded By Photographed by Michael and Rebekah Sheppard on 9 June 2001.
Location Cemeterio Mexicana Las Tres Marias is located in Liberty Hill north of HW29 just west of US 183.
Condition The cemetery was founded 30 Apr 1914 and is still in use. Portions of the cemetery are so heavily overgrown by trees that we were unable to record the markers.
