Immigration16 Oct 1685Philadelphia, PA
Peter Schumacher, widower, arrived in Philadelphia on 16 Oct 1685 on the Francis & Dorothy, from London, Richard Bridgeman, Commander. Among the passengers were Peter Schumacher, and Peter his son, Mary his daughter, and Sarah his Cousin and Frances and Gertrude his daughters. Another daughter, wife of Dielman Kolb, remained and died in Germany.
