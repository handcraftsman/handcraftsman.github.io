Property23 Sep 1843Mercer co., OH
"On 23 Sep 1843 William purchases land in Mercer Co. from Joseph Carlin. (Ref Deed Bk K, pp135-136)."
"The following year, 15 Feb 1844, William and Malinda sell the land to their son, James Downing. (Ref Deed Bk K, pp303-304)."
"On 1 April 1846, Manuel Circle filed a bill in Chancery in the Court of Common Pleas against William, Malinda and James Downing requesting the land be sold to satisfy certain judgements against William Downing. The sheriff of Mercer Co. auctioned the land on 22 June 1850 and the court approved the sale in July court, executed it on 10 Nov 1850 and it was recorded 17 Jan 1851 (Vol P, p31)."
