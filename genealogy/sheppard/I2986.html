Will20 Sep 1870Scotland co., MO
``I Edmund Snow of the county of Scotland in the state of Missouri being of Sound Mind and Memory, and being desirous of setting my worldly affairs while I have cupuerty? so to do. Do make and publish this my last will and testament, that is to say, first I ? all my just debts payed off and discharged as soon as fruiticuble?. Second, I give, devise, and bequeath all of my Estate both Real and Personal to my beloved wife Hannah Snow for and during her natural life and after her death to be equally divided among my heirs after first making Richard B. Snow and Mary H. Snow my only two minor children equal with my elder children in a horse and saddle each when they ? the age twenty one years or shall marry. I will and desire that my wife Hannah Snow act as executrix of this my last will and testament withouout being sec...? to give bond in witness when I h????to set my hand and seal the said county of Scotland on this Day of September 20 1870''
