Will4 Oct 1897Marine twp, Madison Co., IL
"Bartholomew Guntle of Highland, IL, will dated 4 Oct 1897
Last will and testament of Barth. Guntley. In the name of God amen. I, Barth. Guntley, of the town of Marine in the county of Madison, and State of Illinois, of the age of 70 years and being of sound mind and memory do make publish and declare this my last will and testament in the manner following that is to say:
First, that I Bart Guntley bequesth to my wife Veronika Guntley all of my property, which is 30 acres of land, and all the belonging to it, which is situated in Marine township, count of Madison and State of illinois, 20 acres situated in section 26 and 10 acres situated in section 27, in the above township. That the said land shll not be sold by my wife Veronika Guntley while living.
Second, that after the death of my wife Veronika Guntly, my daughter Anna Bargetzi shall be bequesthed all the abovve described land, and that she may dispose of ithe same in any way she deems suitable to herself with the exception that my grandson Christian Bargetzi shall receive dhte sum of $300 in cash but not before he shall become of the age of 21 years, which is Martch 31st 1907.
Third, That I appoint my wife Veronika Guntly and Simon Bargetzi as executors of the above estate without bond.
In witness whereof I have hrerto set my hadn and seal thsi 4th day of October 1897.
signed: Bartholomaus Guntle
witnesses to the above: Simeon Bargetzi, Franklin Beck, both residing at Highland, ILL.
date of death, 7 Oct 1897
date of probate and record of will, 13 Nov 1897."
