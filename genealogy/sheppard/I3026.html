"2 Oct 1736 William Moncrief sold to Isaac Davis for 100 pounds, current money, 160 acres being the plantation and track of land where Wm Muncrief lately lived - so side of North West River, beginning at the river Pequoson, running along by Thomas Muncrief to Daniel Glasgo, Junr to land formerly belonged to John Muncrief.
