Property16 Mar 1836Milford Twp, Butler Co., OH

Property1849Butler Co., OH
Jacob Emerick sold an acre of land for $48.60 to be used for the United Methodist Church, which was dedicated 9 Nov 1850.
Census18 Oct 1850Madison Twp., Butler Co., OH
Oral HistoryAbt 1860
Rev. Jacob Emerick was a very outspoken opponent of slavery, even travelling for the Anti-Salvery Society. He eventually left Pleasant Ridge to join the Christian Union Church, after the conflict ended, a revival brought 60 new members in the church - all of whom were baptized in Elk Creek.
