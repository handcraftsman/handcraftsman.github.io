Events

BirthAbt 1783Wilkes Co., NC
Death23 May 1856Adair Co., KY
SpouseAbt 1800Wilkes Co., NC
m.1st _____ _____ abt 1800 in probably Wilkes co., NC and had: John, Alfred, Lewis, James, and Sally
Member1801Reddies River, Wilkes Co., NC
Andrew Shepherd became a member of the Reddies River Church in 1801. He and his wife "withdrew" from the church in 1814.
Census1810Wilkes Co., NC
Military1812NC
served in the 8th cavalry company detached from the 9th brigade from NC under Capt. Wm. P. Waugh during the war of 1812.
Moved1814Adair Co., KY
moved about 1814 from Wilkes co., NC to Adair co., KY
Tax1815Goose Creek, Adair Co., KY
paid taxes on 100 acres of land on Goose Creek in Adair co., KY in 1815. That section of the county later became part of Russell co., KY
Spouse1 Jul 1819Adair Co., KY
m.2nd Barbara Phillips, dau of John Phillips, on 1 Jul 1819 in Adair co., KY, and had: Delphia, Phoebe, Milton W., Calvin, Preston, Irene, Minerva, Franklin, Olivia, Martha Ann, and Pleasant.
