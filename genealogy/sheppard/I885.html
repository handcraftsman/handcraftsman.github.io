Will19 Nov 1783Bedford Co., VA
will of Richard Hail dated 19 Nov 1783:
``Unto my beloved wife Elizabeth Hail all my personal and real estate during the time of life and while she remains a widow and after her decease to divide equally among my children Sarah Hatcher, John Hail, Elizabeth Hail, James Hail, Martha Hail, Richard Hail, Frank Hail, Powel Hail, Molly Hail, Lewis Hail, Sarah Hatcher one bed and furniture charged with 8 pounds. My son John Hail charged with a horse, bridle and saddle worth 8 pounds. In case any of my children should laive to come of age or to marry to have their portion paid to them out of the Estate equally to the others.
Executors: my beloved wife Elizabeth Hail, Stephen Hail, and my son John Hail and Humphrey Edmundson.''
Witnesses: Richard Edmondson, John Hail, Humphrey Edmondson, John Smith.
Probate28 Jun 1784Bedford Co., VA
will of Richard Hail probated 28 Jun 1784 in Bedford co., Va
by oaths of Richard Edmondson and John Smith.
Executors: Elizabeth Hail and Humphrey Edmondson. Security: Thomas Watts, Richard Edmondson. Liberty being reserved the other Executors therein mentioned to join in the probate thereof when they shall think fit.
