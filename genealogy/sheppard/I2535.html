Probate22 Feb 1883Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
Unto the Surrogate Court of the County of Kent
The petition of James Campbell of the Township of Tilbury East in the County of Kent that Mary Ann Campbell late of the Township of Tilbury East in the County of Kent married woman deceased, died on about the 21st day of May in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and eighty two at Tilbury East in the County of Kent and the said deceased at the time of her death had her fixed place of abode at the Township of Tilbury East in the said County of Kent.
That the said deceased died intestate without having left any Will, Codicil, or Testamentary Paper whatever and that your Petitioner is her husband.
That the personal estate and effects of the said deceased, which she in any way died possessed of, or entitled to, and for and in respect to which Letters of Administration are requested to be granted, are of or about the value of three hundred and sixty six dollars, to the best of your petitioner's knowledge, information and belief.
Wherefore your petitioner prays that administration of the personal estate and effects of the said deceased may be granted and committed to by this Honorable Court.
Dated this 22nd day of February a.d. 1883: signed James Campbell.
