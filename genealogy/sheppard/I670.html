Property6 Sep 1769Camden Dist., SC
"Deed of Conveyance. Augustine Culp and Agnes, his wife, Camden dist. to Andrew Jeter, Greenville co., GA (sic). Whereas 100 acres on W side of Catawba river, Camden dist. granted 4 Oct 1768 to Robert Brown, bounded SW by Matthew's land, on all other sides by vacan lands, plat attached to orig. grant, conveyed 6 Sep 1769 by sd. Robt. Brown to Henry Culp, 26 Acres being part of sd. grant, sold 6 Mar 1775 by Henry Culp to Augustine culp for the sum of 26 pds sterling; now in consideration fo 3 pds sterling money Augustine Culp conveys to Andrew Jeter the above mentioned 26 acres that was part of the 100 acres granted orig. to Robt. Brown, bounded SW and NW by William McKinny's land, on all other sides by Henry Culp's land, plat certified by Andrew McDowell.. signed: Augustine Culp, Agnes Culp. Dated: 28 Jul 1784. Wit: Benjamin Reaves, John Tomlinson, James Massey.
