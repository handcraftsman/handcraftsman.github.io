Obituary18 Dec 1953Chatham, Kent co., Ontario, Canada
"ROSS - Mrs. James, the former Pansy Schaufler, aged 60, of Tilbury, passed away on Friday, December 18, 1953, in Public General Hospital, Chatham. Beloved wife of James Ross, Tilbury. Dear mother of Derwood Ross, Tilbury; Mrs. Gordon Webster (Dorothy), Chatham; Mrs. James Shaw (Jean), Corunna. Dear daughter of Albert Schaufler, Tilbury. Dear sister of Archie, Windsor; Harry, Tilbury; Stewart, Farmington, Mich; Mrs. George Dodson, Comber; Mrs. Gordon Burgess, Toronto; Mrs. Alice Sinclair, Pontiac; Mrs. Keith Mather, London. Five grandchildren. Resting at her late residence, 18 Forrest Avenue, Tilbury, where services will be held on Monday, December 21, at 1:30 p.m., to Darrell S. Moffat Memorial Church, Tilbury, at 2, Rev. F. G. MacKenzie officiating. Interment Stewart Cemetery."
