ObituaryJul 1942LaPlata, Macon Co., MO
``Joseph Moncrief
Joseph Elijah Moncrief was born in Macon County, Missouri March 23, 1870 and died July 19, 1942. He was a son of Robert Wilson and Matilda Moncrief.
He was united in married to Ida Mae Perry January 30, 1896. To this union five sons and one daughter was born. The wife and two children preceded him in death.
On April 1938 he was married to Alice Saling who he leaves to mourn his loss with the four sons who are, Jett, Edgar, Hubert, and Johnny and eight grandchildren. Also five step-children and thirteen step-grandchildren. He also leaves three brothers, Garret, Robert, and Doc; also a brother-in-law, Dora Elsea and family of Wilmathville, Missouri and many nieces and nephews.
In early manhood he united with the Baptist Church at Sue City, Missouri, later years when he came to make his home in LaPlata with his mother, he moved his membership to the Baptist Church in LaPata in which church he remained a member at the time of his death.
LaPlata Home Press July 1942''
