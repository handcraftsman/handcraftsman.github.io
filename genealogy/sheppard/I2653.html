Court1808Pendleton co., KY
``We know from the Pendleton Co., KY, court records that Roswell was not a sterling character. He was called to court in 1808 for assualt and battery and found guilty. Regarding the same incident, three men were paid to guard Rossell (another version of the name) Barton after being served with a warrant. He must have been known to have a bad temper and be a strong man.''
Court1809Pendleton co., KY
``Roswell Barton owed Press G. Kennett, clerk, a fee bill in 1809, and the bill was given to Chas. Sterne, Sheriff, for collection.''
Tax1810<Pendleton co., KY>
"Roswell Barton paid taxes in Kentucky until 1810 and then left the State. Roswell was found guilty of assult and battery in Ky. Was fined 50 dollars two different times Shortly after this he left Ky. owing property taxes"
Moved<1810>OH
listed on the Dec 1810 delinquent tax list for Pendleton co., KY as having moved to Ohio
