Will26 Sep 1878Bosanquet twp, Lambton co., Ontario, Canada
This is the last will and testament of me Dugald Campbell of the Township of Bosanquet in the County of Lambton of the Province of Ontario.
Farmer
First I desire that all my debts and funeral expenses shall be paid out of my personal estate.
Second I will and bequest to my beloved wife Mary Ann Campbell the residue of my personal estate of whatever kind or nature I may die possessed of to have and hold to my said wife Mary Ann her heirs and assigns forever.
Third I will, devise and bequeath to my said wife Mary Ann Campbell all my real estate consisting of first the South half of Lot number Three in the Seventh Concession of the Township of Brooke being one hundred acres and second and all that portion of rights, title and interest either possession or expectance of fifty acres of the West half of Lot Nine? in the Third Concession of the Township of Bosanquet in the said County of Lambton. All in the Province of Ontario being the land bequeathed to me from my father John Campbell of Bosanquet and in accordance with certain agreements made between him and myself and for work and labor done and performed by me. To have and to hold be the aforesaid and all my interests thereof to my said wife Mary Ann Campbell, her heirs and assigns forever.
And lastly I hereby nominate and appoint my said wife Mary Ann Campbell sole executrix of this my last will and testament.
As witness my hand and seal at the residence of Benjamin Bell in the Township of Harwich in the County of Kent this twenty sixth day of September in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and seventy eight. In the presence of Malcolm Bell of Harwich, farmer. John Duck, esq. Signed Dugald Campbell.
