Property1877Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
"At the age of twenty-two he located on the farm taken up by his grandfather, and after a residence of sixteen years purchased 120 acres on the Back Line, where since 1896 he has had his home. Mr. Campbell owns (1904) considerable land and engages largely in stock farming, making a specialty of export cattle."
