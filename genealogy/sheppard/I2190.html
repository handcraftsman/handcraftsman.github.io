Oral HistoryBridle Creek, Grayson Co., VA
"..some of the early settlers that lived up and down [Bridle] creek. Joshua Cox lived below the Fields place; his land joined Fields. he had a family of five noted sons and three daughters: Hardin, David, John, Samuel, and Isom; Jennie, his oldest daughter, married Charles Doughton; Hannah married Solomon Osborne; the other one (Ruth, I think was her name) married Ben Ward."
