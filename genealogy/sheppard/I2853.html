MentionedFeb 1755Currituck co., NC
Elizabeth Muncreef (sic) was mentioned in her father's will dated Feb 1755 in Currituck co., NC
Property22 Jun 1766Currituck co., NC
"Josiah Moncreef sold land to William Moncreef which Josiah, his wife Elizabeth and mother Elizabeth, a widow, owned excepting acreage sold by William Moncreef the elder to William Wililamson. Deed of gift from Josiah, Elizabeth, and Elizabeth, June 22 1766. Thomas Moncreef was a witness reg. Dec 29 1766."
Property5 Feb 1774Halifax co., NC
"Elizabeth Muncreef on February 5, 1774 bought land on the south side of Burnt Coat Swamp in Southern Halifax county form Lewis and Jemima Pope. the acreage was 120 acres, more or less. Sampson Muncreef made his mark as Elizabeth witness. Halifax conveyances. Sampson and Josiah soon appear thereafter in Georgia documents. They were among the Revolutionary soldiers of that state."
