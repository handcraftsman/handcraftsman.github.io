Announcing the publication of

 

The Needels Family Of Northwest Missouri

 

Volume 1

The Descendants Of Cubbage Barrett Needels

 

This is the first of a series that will follow the descendants of each of the seven children of Alexander and Elizabeth (Cubbage) Needels of Morrow County, Ohio. All but one of these children migrated to northwest Missouri, when they reached adulthood.

 This book is based principally upon letters, documents, and pictures in the possession of Cubbage's descendants, and upon their memories of stories told by their elders. There is a biographical sketch, using available information, for each descendant, with an accompanying photograph, if available.

 Family names, other than Needels, that are prominent in this book include Binns, Brodmerkle, Clark, Cooper, Drinkwater, Humpert, Lynn, Stevens, Stwalley, Webb, and Wimp.

 

·        6" x 9" book size, hard bound with gold stamping

·        887 pages, including 1,792 entries in a name index. Women are listed under both their birth and their married names, where appropriate

·        A chart showing the descent from Alexander Needels for each descendant

·        791 photographs

·        Published by Gateway Press, Inc., Baltimore, MD

 

The $85.00 includes packaging and shipping.
