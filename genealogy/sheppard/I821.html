ObituaryNov 1936<MO>
"Mary Ann Boyd was born Jan 20, 1862 in Webster co., Mo. The daughter of Ira and Mary Evelyn (Alexander). On Sept 11, 1884 she was married to Joseph Beverly Boyd and to this union four children were born. One of whom, William Ira Boyd preceeded her in death on Sep 9, 1928. The others survive her.
Mr. Boyd preceeded her in death on June 21, 1923.
A sister, Mrs. Tom Day, also preceeded her in death on Feb 23, 1923.
Fifty four years ago she was converted and joined the "New Bethel Primative Baptist Church", to which she remained a faithful member until her death.
For a number of years she has had the honor of being the oldest member of the church.
She passed away on Monday A.M. Nov 2, 1936 at 5 min past 9am in the home where she had spent her entire life. She was 74 years , 9 months, and 13 days of age.
Those who survive her are:
One daughter - Mrs. Gertrude Hendrix of Springfield, Mo
Two sons - Baxter Boyd of Winona, Mo and Hugh Boyd of the home.
One sister - Mrs. Missouri (Joe) Dill, of Niangua, Mo
Seven grand children and a large number of other relatives and friends."
