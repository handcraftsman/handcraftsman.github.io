Events

Birth1809<Ballymoney, County Antrim, Ireland>
Death1891<Butler Co., IA>
BuriedOak Hill Cemetery, Parkersburg, Butler co., IA
Property10 Jun 1847Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
Andrew Campbell was patented 100 acres comprising N 1/2 of Lot 10 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp by the Crown on 10 Jun 1847.
"Andrew took up government land, the north half of Lot 10 M.R.N, and married Bridget Finnessey."
Spouse<1840-1850>Harwich, Kent co., Ontario, Canada
m. Bridget Belinda Finnessey (1819 in Ireland - 1874 Iowa), dau of John Fennessey and Bridget _____ abt 1840 in Harwich, Kent co., Ontario and had at least: Belinda (1852), Grace Ann (1855), Edward (1856), and Martha (1860)
Court4 Feb 1853Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
As the executor for his father, Andrew Campbell extr and eldest son of Charles Campbell sold 100 acres comprising N 1/2 of Lot 9 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp to [his brothers] William H. Campbell (E 1/2 of N 1/2 50 acres) and Charles Campbell (W 1/2 of N 1/2 50 acres) for L25 each on 4 Feb 1853.
Property21 Feb 1860Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
Andrew Campbell bought 70 acres comprising SE part of Lot 6 Con 9 of Tilbury East twp from Henry Waters for $75 on 21 Feb 1860.
Court31 May 1860Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
As the executor for his father, Andrew sold 100 acres comprising N part of Lot 6 Con 9 of Tilbury East twp to Charles Farguharson for $400 on 31 May 1860.
Moved<1856-1860>IL
[moved from Tilbury East twp., Kent co., Ontario to Illinois based on b. of ch Edward and Martha --gjr]
Census26 Jun 1860Groveland, LaSalle co., IL
Property12 Sep 1863Tilbury East Twp., Kent co., Ontario, Canada
Andrew Campbell sold 70 acres comprising SE part of Lot 6 Con 9 of Tilbury East twp to John Fletcher for $1000 on 12 Sep 1863.
Andrew Campbell sold 100 acres comprising N 1/2 of Lot 10 of North Middle Road in Tilbury East twp to John Kerr for $1000 on 12 Sep 1863.
